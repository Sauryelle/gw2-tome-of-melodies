A beautiful, cross-platform desktop application designed for musicians in *Guild Wars 2* and *Heartopia*. Scribe, organize, and manage your custom music tabs with a clean, thematic interface.

Companion app to [Sarya's Virtual Piano](https://sauryelle.github.io/gw2-virtual-piano/).

## âœ¨ Features
* **Thematic UI:** Fully stylized to match the aesthetic of Tyria.
* **Smart Scribe Highlighting:** Automatically color-codes your syntax. `(Notes)` glow red, and `[Notes]` glow blue for easy reading.
* **Local Storage:** Your tabs are saved automatically to your machine.
* **Tome Backup:** Easily Export your entire songbook as a JSON file to share with friends, or Import shared songbooks to merge them into your collection.
* **Cross-Platform:** Available for both Windows (.exe) and Linux (.AppImage).
